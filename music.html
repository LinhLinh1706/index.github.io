INDIE

Indie is a genre of music in which independent artists release it with no need of permission from label. Indie is also known as underground musics.

This is a playlist where I gather all the indie songs that I enjoy listening. Artists are various from Korean, American, Vietnamese, etc. Playlist will get updated throughout the year.
